FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

EVERYTHING YOU NEED TO KNOW

Is Soccer Superstar free to play?

Yes — Soccer Superstar is completely free to download and play on both iOS and Android. The game is supported by optional in-app purchases and ads.

Can I play without an internet connection?

Absolutely. Soccer Superstar is designed for offline play — including Leagues, Tournaments, and Training modes. Just download it once and enjoy anytime, anywhere.

What devices is it available on?

Soccer Superstar is available on Android (Google Play) and iOS (App Store). It's rated Everyone, making it suitable for players of all ages.

How do the controls work?

It's a simple flick-to-kick mechanic — just swipe your finger across the ball to shoot. The direction, speed, and curve of your swipe determines the flight of the ball. No buttons, no joysticks.

How do I unlock new players?

Play through the main levels to earn reward packs and collect player shards. Once you've gathered enough shards for a footballer, they're yours to add to your squad.

What is the Daily Challenge?

The Daily Challenge is a limited-time event that rolls out periodically — roughly once a month. Each event comes with its own themed stadium and exclusive gear, so the whole experience matches the event's vibe. Complete it before time runs out to claim the rewards.
